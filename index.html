How Much Storage Does Popular Media Need?

📺 The Simpsons Complete Series

The Simpsons has over 35 seasons and hundreds of episodes.

Estimated storage:

1-5 TB

Depending on quality:

  • Low quality streaming files: ~1TB
  • HD episodes: ~3TB
  • Very high quality archive: 5TB+

📖 One Piece Manga Collection

The One Piece manga contains more than 100 volumes.

A digital collection estimate:

2-10 GB

depending on scan quality and image resolution.

🎬 One Piece Anime

The anime contains over 1000 episodes.

Estimated storage:

500 GB - 5 TB

  • Compressed quality: ~500GB
  • HD archive: 2-5TB+

⚡ Harry Potter Books

The seven Harry Potter novels in text format require very little space.

Less than 10 MB

Even with covers and formatting, the collection is usually under 100MB.
